Extermination Plan

Here is your guide to ridding yourself from the clutches of the evil roach

Step #1

Exterior spraying

Spray ground around exterior perimiter of house, where the walls meet the floor with insectiside. In addition to this, spray around perimiter of windows.

Step #2

Exterior barrior check

Check for any gaps that open the inside of your house to the outdoors, and block them off. i.e Broken windows, gaps in doors, or holes in screens.

Small gaps can be filled with Acrylic Latex Caulk. (aka Painter's Caulk) It is suitable for both indoors and outdoors, and is commonly used as a sealant around windows and doors.

Step #3

Clean your kitchen thoroughly

Give your kitchen a deep thorough clean.

  • Wipe all surfaces
  • Clean beneath and behind appliances
  • Clean cupboard doors
  • Sweep
  • Mop

Step #4

Bug bombs

Set off a Bug Bomb in the kitcen, and in your attic/ceiling void.
Make sure all cupboard doors are open and there is unrestricted airflow, to all parts of the room the bomb will be working in.

Step #5

Gels and indoor spray spray

Use gels in corners and edges where roacheslike to gather and hide. Also you can use a spray to accross surfaces that you can't reach, like beneath cupboards, behind fridges, and under the stove.

Now you have done most of what is reasonable to do.

Roaches tend to prefer dark, moist places to hide and breed and can be found behind refrigerators, sinks and stoves, as well as under floor drains and inside of motors and major appliances.

Step #6

Precautions

Now that you have done the drastic measures you need to be careful. The best way to get rid of roaches, is to prevent getting them in the first place.

To help prevent roaches, is to keep a clean tidy house. Make sure at the end of the you check:

  • No dishes in the sink
  • No food is left out
  • Benches are clean
  • Floors are swept
  • Trash is taken out

If you do this there is a much smaller chance of getting roaches in the first place.

Roaches like clutter. If you reduce clutter in your house, there will be less inviting dark nooks and crannies for roaches to hide, and if there is a roach you will have an easier time seeing it, and you will be able to take action straight away.

Stay clean and stay Roach free.
